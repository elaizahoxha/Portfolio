My Workflow

Discover
Talk to users, map journeys, and identify friction. Turn ambiguity into clear, testable problem statements.
Define
Frame the problem, align on success metrics, assess constraints, and prioritize the few bets that matter most.
Design
Sketch → wireframe → high-fidelity prototypes. Validate assumptions quickly through feedback and lightweight testing.
Develop
Build accessible, responsive solutions; integrate APIs; collaborate closely with engineering while keeping scope realistic.
Deliver
Ship, measure impact, and learn. Translate results into insights that inform the next sprint, roadmap, or release.
